About Us

Precision. Discretion. Resilience.

PANVENEDUX delivers strategic consultancy designed for leaders and organisations operating under real pressure — where clarity, governance, communication, and crisis resilience aren't optional; they're critical.

Founded by Mario Petravo, PANVENEDUX brings the discipline of international strategy, leadership development, and frontline operational advisory into a sharp, high-impact model for today’s complex world.

Our Work

Our expertise is rooted in deep advisory experience across finance, defense, entertainment, and international policy sectors. We specialise in preparing executive clients for appearances and strategic engagements at some of the world’s most prestigious platforms, including the Venice Film Festival, Cannes Film Festival, BAFTA Film Awards, BAFTA TV Awards, US Academy Awards, Olivier Awards, and The Proms. Our work has supported client participation at gatherings such as the 10th Anniversary of the Invictus Games, the Grouse & Grape Luncheon at Spencer House, the Gulls’ Luncheon at Spencer House, and the BAFTA Oscar Nominees Party at the British Consul General’s Residence in Los Angeles.

We have prepared executive clients for strategic participation at key industry and diplomatic events, including their appearances at the HollyShorts Oscar-Qualifying Festival, the British Consul General’s Farewell Reception in Hancock Park, and exclusive gatherings during the Cannes and Venice Film Festivals.

We also prepare clients for high-profile speaking and panel opportunities, including the groundbreaking MOSAIC 2024 conference at Royaumont Abbey in France, and formal sessions hosted by the UK Parliamentary Society for Arts, Fashion, and Sports. Our presence extends to private events such as Breaking Through The Lens and Producers Without Borders, further supporting independent film and creative industry leadership.

Through every project, PANVENEDUX ensures strategic alignment, stakeholder clarity, and communication precision — empowering clients to navigate high-visibility environments with confidence and impact.

Beyond high-profile cultural and diplomatic events, we also have experience organising exclusive investor gatherings and private family office lunches in Jersey, London, the Cotswolds, and Monaco. Our The Ton events bring together a carefully selected and relevant group of investors in a convivial setting, enabling engaging and substantive investment presentations. The goal: increased opportunity, cooperation, and real connection.

These events are not about pitching products — they’re about presenting authentic, topical, and insightful ideas. Speakers are drawn from finance, media, and technology sectors to educate, challenge, and inspire.

We have also contributed to complex deal environments, including Fabergé-linked transactions where we worked in close alignment with established experts, and inter-state carbon transfer agreements, leveraging our discretion, trust networks, and policy insight to support secure and meaningful outcomes.
