UX Analysis and End-to-End Journey Assessment

In addition to strategic and communications consulting, PANVENEDUX provides comprehensive UX analysis and user experience evaluation — from the first point of contact to the completion of the full user journey. This expertise supports organisations seeking greater clarity, efficiency, and trust in how clients, partners, and teams interact with their systems, processes, and digital products.

Our UX End-to-End Journey Assessment integrates strategic analysis, behavioural research, and measurable usability indicators. Beyond the System Usability Score (SUS), we employ the User Experience Questionnaire (UEQ), Single Ease Question (SEQ), Net Promoter Score (NPS), and Task Success Rate (TSR) to create a multi-dimensional view of performance and satisfaction. Using heatmap analysis, A/B testing, cognitive walkthroughs, and heuristic evaluations (based on Nielsen's principles), we identify friction points and opportunities to optimise user flows.

We conduct journey mapping, service blueprinting, and information architecture evaluations to ensure alignment between strategic goals, design, and user experience. When relevant, we incorporate eye-tracking analysis, card sorting, and UX maturity assessments to build lasting frameworks for improvement, beyond one-off usability studies.

Our approach is human-centred and systemic — blending business strategy, design thinking, and behavioural science. Each assessment results in a clear, actionable plan, empowering clients to achieve greater consistency, clarity, and confidence across every interaction.
