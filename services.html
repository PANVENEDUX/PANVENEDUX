UX & Experience

End-to-End User Experience

01

End‑to‑End Journey Assessment

Full-spectrum mapping from first touch to completion, identifying friction, cognitive load, and trust gaps across priority flows. Outputs include a sequenced improvement plan tied to business outcomes.

02

Usability Measurement (SUS, UEQ, SEQ, NPS, TSR)

Quant and qual measurement stack to benchmark usability and satisfaction, compare cohorts, and track change over time. Integrated dashboards align UX signals with operational KPIs.

03

Heuristic Reviews & Cognitive Walkthroughs

Expert evaluation based on Nielsen's heuristics, error prevention, recognition vs. recall, and progressive disclosure. Cognitive walkthroughs validate task clarity for first-time and expert users.

04

Journey Mapping & Service Blueprinting

Layered mapping of user intent, backstage processes, and handoffs to ensure the service model supports the intended experience. Delivers cross-team alignment artefacts.

05

Information Architecture & Card Sorting

Evaluate and restructure navigation, taxonomy, and labeling. Open/closed card sorting and tree-testing to reduce time-to-find and decision fatigue.

06

Experimentation & Behavioural Analytics

A/B testing, heatmaps, and funnel analysis; optional eye-tracking studies for high-stakes flows. Synthesised insights convert into prioritized hypotheses and rollout plans.

07

UX Maturity & Governance

Maturity assessment, operating model design, RACI and cadence for decisions, and definition of UX success metrics that leadership can own and track.
